From: Mohammed Sulieman, Senior Data Analyst
1. Executive Summary
The Retail Performance Dashboard indicates a strong Year-over-Year growth trajectory, with total revenue reaching 19.45M, However, operational inefficiencies are detected in the form of a 7.28
1.53M and a significant segment of "At-Risk" customers who have not transacted in over 6 months.

2. Key Findings
Revenue Growth: The business displays strong seasonality, with Q4 (Sept-Dec) driving approximately 35% of annual revenue. Q4 2011 shows a slowing growth trend compared to the explosive growth seen in 2010.
Product Portfolio: Pareto analysis confirms that top 100 products drive the majority of volume. However, several high-volume items are suffering from above-average return rates (>5%), indicating potential quality control or description errors.
Customer Health: While we have 5,940 active customers, nearly 25% of revenue comes from "Guest" (Unknown) checkouts. Among identified customers, a cohort of high-value clients has become dormant (Churned) prior to June 2011.
3. Strategic Recommendations
Reduce Returns: Launch a quality audit on the "Top 10 Returned Products" identified in the Product Analysis view. Reducing the return rate by just 1% would reclaim $200k+ in annual revenue.
Customer Activation: The Marketing team should target the "At-Risk" list (identified in the dashboard) with re-engagement offers. These customers have a proven history of high spend but have gone cold.
Guest Conversion: Implement a loyalty incentive at checkout to capture the "Unknown" customer data. We are currently blind to 25% of our customer behaviors.
